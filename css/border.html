We were six friends who went to Cox's Bazar. This was a tremendous journey for all us. All we did is fun. We travelled through bus there. On day 1, we went to the beach, had different type of unique foods, jammed with music, danced with tune and clicked so many pictures. We girls wore saree on the beach which got ticked my another bucket list. On day 2, we went to the mountains and visited beaches called Jhautola Beach and Kakra Beach.My whole 2025 went so well but this was the highlighted part.
